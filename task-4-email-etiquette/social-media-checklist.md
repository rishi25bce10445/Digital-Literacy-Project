#### ***5 Do's \& 5 Don'ts for Responsible social media use as a college student:***



### *5 Do's:*

* **Maintain a professional profile and username.**
* **Think before posting any content.**
* **Use strong and unique passwords.**
* **Verify information before sharing.**
* **Respect others' opinions and privacy.**





### *5 Don'ts:*

* **Do not share personal or sensitive information.**
* **Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.**
* **Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.**
* **Avoid spreading misinformation.**
* **Do not engage in online arguments or harassment.**
