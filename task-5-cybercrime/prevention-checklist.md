#### **"Stay safe online" Prevention Checklist:**



1. Never approve UPI payment requests unless you are sure you are paying someone.
2. Always verify the sender before making any digital payment.
3. Do not share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone.
4. Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.
5. Use only official and trusted payment applications.
6. Double cheque QR codes and payment details before scanning.
7. Enable app notifications to track all transactions instantly.
8. Do not trust messages offering rewards refunds or urgent payments.
9. Report any suspicious transaction right away.
